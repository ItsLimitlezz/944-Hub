## Introduction

The following fuel system line diagram includes most of the vacuum line routing for the 1985.5 and newer 8V Normally-Aspirated 944s. However, there is additional vacuum line routing for the heater control valve and ventilation controls. That vacuum line routing is similar for all 944 models and will be included in a drawing to be developed in the near future.
