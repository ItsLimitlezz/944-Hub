## Tools

- Metric Socket / Wrench Set
- Floor Jack / Jack Stands

## Procedure

1. Raise the vehicle on jack stands.
2. Removing the rear wheels makes the job a bit easier but is not required.
3. Remove the two bolts/nuts on either end of the sway bar that attach the sway bar to the drop links.![Rear Sway Bar Removal and Installation](/articles/rear-sway-bar-removal-and-installation/rear_stabilizer_drop_link_1.jpg)
4. Remove the bolts (4 - 2 on each side) that hold the sway bar to the torque tube carrier and remove the sway bar from the vehicle.![Rear Sway Bar Removal and Installation](/articles/rear-sway-bar-removal-and-installation/rear_stabilizer_bolts_1.jpg)
5. Installation is the reverse of removal.
