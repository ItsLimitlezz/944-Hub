## Tools

- Metric Wrench Set
- Metric Socket Set
- Jack Stands
- Floor Jack

## Procedure

1. Raise the front of the vehicle on jack stands.
2. Remove the belly pan.
3. Remove the two bolts that attach each stabilizer mounting bar to the body.
4. Remove the bushing retainers that attach the stabilizer bar to the control arms.
5. Lower the stabilizer bar from the vehicle.
6. Installation is the reverse of removal.
