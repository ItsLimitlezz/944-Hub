A single place to look up the fasteners you'll meet on a 944, 944 Turbo, 924S or 968. The specs here are pulled from the individual repair guides — each section links back to the procedure it came from, where you'll find the full context, tools and sequence.

> **Before you reach for the torque wrench.** Values vary by model year and by whether a fastener is reused — always confirm against the specific procedure for your car. Where the original gave only one unit, the converted value is marked with **≈**. Angle-tightening (e.g. *+60°*) means turn the fastener the stated angle *after* the initial torque, using a paint mark to track it.

## General fastener torque

When a specific value isn't called out, these are the default torques by thread size:

| Thread size | Torque |
| --- | --- |
| 6 mm | 8 Nm (6 ft-lb) |
| 8 mm | 20 Nm (15 ft-lb) |
| 10 mm | 40 Nm (29 ft-lb) |
